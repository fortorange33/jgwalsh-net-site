Welcome

Welcome to jgwalsh.net. This site highlights my expertise in psychology, data analysis, and law enforcement readiness.

I bring over three years of experience as a mental health specialist at McLean Hospital, specifically within the McLean-Franciscan unit, a psychiatric hospital in Brighton, Massachusetts affiliated with Harvard Medical School. My work encompassed clinical assessment, crisis intervention, and interdisciplinary collaboration in acute psychiatric settings.

My academic foundation in psychology—spanning undergraduate and graduate-level training—is complemented by advanced quantitative and analytical skills, particularly in SPSS, R, and Excel.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, I returned to Albany, New York, and witnessed significant shifts in clinical psychology and mental health systems, including the rise of teletherapy. These changes inspired me to explore alternative career paths, leveraging my expertise to address critical challenges in federal or state law enforcement. This journey reflects my commitment to public safety and community well-being.

Core Competencies

  • Behavioral Assessment and Crisis Management
  • Interdisciplinary Team Collaboration
  • Quantitative Analysis and Data Interpretation (SPSS, R, Excel)
  • Application of Psychological Principles
  • Critical Thinking and Problem-Solving in High-Stakes Environments
