Welcome

Welcome to jgwalsh.net, the official professional portfolio of James Gregory Walsh (Greg). I combine over five years of clinical experience in mental health counseling with a strong analytical skill set focused on public safety, behavioral assessment, and data-driven decision-making. I am currently transitioning into federal or state law enforcement.

My work has centered on crisis intervention, interdisciplinary collaboration, and behavioral risk assessment, often involving psychiatrists, psychologists, and social workers in acute inpatient settings. Complemented by graduate training in clinical psychology and advanced data analytics using tools like SPSS, R, and Excel, I bring both human and technical insight to the field of public safety and law enforcement.

Key Competencies

  • Crisis intervention and de-escalation
  • Behavioral threat assessment and evaluation
  • Interdisciplinary collaboration in clinical settings
  • Quantitative analysis using SPSS, R, and Excel
  • Public service, law enforcement readiness, and civic resilience
