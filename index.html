Welcome

Welcome to jgwalsh.net. I have over three years of experience as a mental health specialist at McLean Hospital, specifically within the McLean-Franciscan unit, a psychiatric hospital in Brighton, Massachusetts affiliated with Harvard Medical School. My work involved clinical assessment, crisis intervention, and interdisciplinary collaboration within acute psychiatric settings.

My educational foundation in psychology—spanning undergraduate and graduate-level training—is enhanced by strong quantitative and analytical capabilities, particularly using SPSS, R, and Excel.

Moving back to Albany, NY, during the COVID-19 pandemic coincided with significant changes in the field of psychology, notably the rapid expansion of remote teletherapy. Witnessing these shifts prompted me to reconsider my professional trajectory and explore how my background might align with alternative career paths. This reflection led me to pursue opportunities in federal or state law enforcement, aiming to contribute toward addressing critical challenges facing both local communities and the broader national landscape.

Core Competencies

  • Interdisciplinary Collaboration and Communication
  • Advanced Quantitative Analysis (SPSS, R, Excel)
  • Applied Psychological Principles
